# Privacy Policy — BYOB Map

**Effective date:** July 1, 2026

Small Tools Studio ("we," "us," or "our") operates the BYOB Map mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices.

## Summary

BYOB Map does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not sell or share data with third parties. The App's only data-related permission is device location, which is used solely to show you nearby BYOB restaurants and is never sent to or stored on our servers.

## Information We Access

**Location.** If you grant location permission, the App uses your device's GPS coordinates to center the map view and calculate which restaurants are nearest to you. This calculation happens entirely on your device. We do not transmit, log, or store your location on any server. You can deny or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings; the App remains fully usable without it (you simply won't see distance-based sorting or map centering).

**Restaurant data.** The App displays a public list of Philadelphia BYOB restaurants (name, address, phone, cuisine type, corkage fee policy) served from our Firebase Firestore database and Firebase Storage (for images). This data is read-only and is not connected to you personally — the App has no accounts, so there is no way to associate this data with an individual user.

**We do not collect:**
- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Photos, contacts, or files from your device
- Payment or financial information
- Any user-generated content (there are no reviews, ratings, or accounts in this App)

## Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how the App is used so we can improve it. This is automatically collected and is separate from the precise GPS location described above (which is never sent to us). Google Analytics for Firebase may collect:

- App usage data (screens viewed, features used, session length, button taps)
- Device and app information (device model, OS version, app version, language)
- An anonymous, app-specific installation identifier
- Approximate location derived from IP address (city/region level only — not precise GPS)

This data is processed by Google in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [how Google uses data from Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy). It is not linked to any account, since the App has none, and cannot be used to identify you personally.

We also use Firebase Performance Monitoring to measure technical performance such as app startup time, screen rendering speed, and network request latency. This data is anonymous, aggregated, and used only to help us identify and fix slow or broken parts of the App — it is not linked to you personally.

We do not currently use Firebase Crashlytics or any other crash-reporting tool (confirmed via Firebase Console, 2026-07-01). If this changes in the future, we will update this policy to disclose what crash data is collected.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to function:
- **Google Firebase** (Firestore database and Storage) — to serve restaurant listings and images
- **Google Maps** — to open turn-by-turn navigation when you tap "Get Directions" (this opens the Google Maps app or website directly; we do not receive data from that interaction)

These services may have their own data practices; see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on Firebase and Google Maps.

## Children's Privacy

BYOB Map is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children, consistent with the App containing no accounts or personal data collection of any kind.

## Data Retention & Deletion

Because the App does not collect or store any personal or location data on our servers, there is no user data to retain or delete.
