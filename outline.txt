The policy should be visible across key touchpoints: printed on the menu, available on your website, and explained by staff when appropriate. 

A well-structured corkage policy should include:
Whether corkage is allowed
The fee per bottle
Any bottle limits (e.g., one or two bottles)
Exceptions or waivers (e.g., waived fee if the bottle isn’t on your wine list or if the guest orders from your list as well)
